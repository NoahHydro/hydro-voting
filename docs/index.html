Block Tank 2018

Decentralized Voting via Smart Contract

Welcome to Block Tank 2018!

Today's winners will be chosen by smart contract. The QR code you recieved at the door encodes your secret, unique ballot. This is what will let cast your vote with this DApp! Simply scan the image with any QR code reader (most modern smartphones let you do this with your camera), and enter the number into the 'Secret Ballot' field below.

Since voting requires you to send a transaction to the Ethereum blockchain, we've partnered with Trust Wallet to make this possible. Simply fill out the fields below, and click 'Vote'!

Secret Ballot
The secret encoded within your QR code
Candidate ID
